UNIFORM

Our pupils are required to come In proper school uniform to the school. The school uniform for boys/girls Is as follows-.

In summer (April to September)

Boys: (i) Blue Half/Full Pant (ii) Orange Shirt (iii) Black Shoes, (iv) Blue Socks (v) Tie, Belt, Batch Girls: (i) Blue Skirt/Midy (ii) Orange Shirt (iii) Black Shoes, (iv) Full Length Blue Socks (v) Tie, Belt, Batch, Band, Rubber.

In Winter (October to March)

(i) Blue Half / Full Plain & ‘V’ Shape Sweater. (ii) Scarp/ Woolen Cap. Boy's hair should be oiled and neatly combed. Girls hair should be oiled and wear two pleated ponies .Wearing jewellery In school Is not allowed and girls can only wear stud earrings (one In each ear) If they have their ears pierced.
