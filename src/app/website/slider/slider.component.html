About Us

Welcome To Swami Vivekanand Shiksha Niketan School
Best Education in Our School.
SVSN Junior/Senior School is a Co-Educational English Medium School having classes from Nursery to Std. XII. It was established in 1998 and has been administrated by Pratibha Vikash Kendra registered under the trust Reg. Act. 1882 in the chairmanship of Mrs. Pratibha Singh. SVSN Junior / Senior School is located at Om Shanti Chowk, Ghoswar, Hajipur, Vaishali, Bihar (844101) a healthy and peaceful locality of Hajipur. The location of the school is very congenial having a friendly and peaceful neighborhood. The spot is immaculate, hygienic and completely pollution free having abundance of green surrounding and more than three acres of its own land with protected campus.

Laboratries

Computer Labs are upgraded every year to keep up the labs with the latest technologies. All computers have latest facilities. Each student is allotted a separate computer to do the practicals. Demonstrations are given in the demonstration room, where teaching is done with the help of LCD projector. The school has well equipped laboratories for Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Computer Science. Keeping in view the latest curriculum, labs for Mathematics,& fine Arts, and have also been developed.

Digital Class Rooms

To keep pace with the changing scenario of education in the present virtual times, the school has smart class rooms furnished with projectors and advanced software to facilitate teaching of various subjects. The school has introduced unique 3rd Generation Smart Class rooms with animated course contents to make the teaching more innovative, effective and interesting.
